## Overview

This project tracks the setup of my academic website.
The immediate goal is to keep the site easy to maintain while supporting future research pages, application materials, and technical writing.

## Design Principles

- Markdown-first content editing
- Minimal deployment friction with GitHub Pages
- Clear separation between static pages and repeatable content types
- Easy future expansion for publications, projects, and notes
